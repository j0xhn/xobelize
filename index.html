Frequently Asked Questions

Is this real?

Absolutely. We saw this blog post about fractional ownership and decided to do it differently. At first it may seem a little far fetched, but it makes a lot of sense and the more you learn about it the more you'll love it.

Are the packages above the only ones?

We offer packages for nearly any interested group size. This includes multiple payment options and in-between packages.

When will the lot and cabin be ready to visit?

The land is primed, as soon as we close funding we should have a cabin in 6-9 months. However, other cabins are available immediately if you would like to come take a look and see the community and land for yourself before purchase.

What Does Access Year Round Imply?

It means that this land will be shared amoung a few groups. If your package hasn't been reserved two weeks in advance you are free to reserve it for as long as you wish. This comes with a monthly "caretaker" fee of $9.

Have you been to the land yourself?

Yes of course! We met some of the community memebers, and had a really good time with the guy incharge of the whole community.

Why the yearly fee?

The up-front cost is the one-time purchase price for the package. We need annual payments for regular maintenance and care of the cabin and our contribution to community projects.
